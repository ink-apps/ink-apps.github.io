üöÄ Getting Started

What is ausp.ink?

ausp.ink is a Shopify app that lets you export orders in an Australia Post-compatible format. It helps streamline the process of generating shipping labels through MyPost Business or eParcel.

How do I access ausp.ink in Shopify?

You can access ausp.ink from the Shopify order list. Just select one or more orders and choose "Export to Australia Post" from the "More actions" menu.

Do I need an Australia Post account to use the app?

Yes, you'll need a MyPost Business or eParcel account to upload the exported file and generate shipping labels.

Does the app work for international orders?

ausp.ink is currently focused on domestic shipping within Australia. International support may be added in the future.

üì¶ Order Export & Shipping Labels

What format does ausp.ink export to?

The app exports orders to a CSV file that's compatible with both MyPost Business and eParcel systems.

Can I export multiple orders at once?

Yes, ausp.ink supports bulk exports so you can save time and handle high-volume shipments efficiently.

Can I export fulfilled orders?

The app is designed for unfulfilled or partially fulfilled orders, as fulfilled orders typically don't require new shipping labels.

Does the app generate the shipping labels for me?

Not directly. ausp.ink prepares an import-ready file that you upload to your Australia Post account to generate the labels.

üìê Parcel Configuration

Can I customise parcel details during export?

Yes, you can adjust size, weight, and shipping preferences for each order before export.

What are custom parcels, and how do they work?

Custom parcels let you predefine commonly used parcel types (e.g., "Standard Box", "Express Satchel"). These can be reused during export for consistency and speed.

Can I create and manage multiple custom parcel types?

Absolutely! You can define as many parcel types as your business needs and apply them during the export process.
