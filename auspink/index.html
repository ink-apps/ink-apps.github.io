Main Features

Seamless AusPost Export

Easily exports orders from Shopify to Australia Post.

Small Business Ready

Works with every MyPost account, no need for the enterprise plan.

Easy Configuration

Set up parcels you use regularly, and choose them during export.

Bulk Import in AusPost

Download a CSV file ready to be bulk imported into Australia Post.

Fulfill Orders

After creating shipping labels, easily fulfill all exported orders in the app.

How to Get Started

  1. Install: Find ausp.ink in the Shopify App Store and click install.
  2. Configure: Set up your business details and parcels.
  3. Export: Export orders directly from your Shopify orders list.
  4. Import: Import your configured shipments into Australia Post and generate labels.
  5. Fulfill: Fulfill all exported orders in the ausp.ink app.
