Main Features

Seamless Xero Sync

Sync orders, invoices and payments to Xero in real time, no manual exports required.

Accurate Tax Mapping

Configure your tax rates to map correctly between platforms.

Support for Discounts and Refunds

Handle discounts and refunds, ensuring your financial records remain accurate.

Payment Type Mapping

Map and configure payment types to fit your Xero setup.

Error Handling

Smart alerts and logging to help you resolve sync issues quickly.

How to Get Started

  1. Install: Find x.ink in the Shopify App Store and click install.
  2. Connect: Authorize your Xero account securely.
  3. Configure: Set up your tax rules and payment type mappings.
  4. Go Live: Enable auto-sync and see your invoices appear in Xero!
