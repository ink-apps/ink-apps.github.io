Main Features

Seamless Xero Sync

Automatically sync your Shopify orders with Xero, eliminating manual data entry.

Accurate Tax Mapping

Configure your tax rates to map correctly between platforms.

Multi-currency Support

Handle international orders with built-in currency conversion support.

Error Handling

Smart alerts and logging to help you resolve sync issues quickly.

Custom Rules

Define rules for invoice generation and account mapping to fit your workflow.

How to Get Started

  1. Install: Find x.ink in the Shopify App Store and click install.
  2. Connect: Authorize your Xero account securely.
  3. Configure: Set up your tax rules and invoice settings.
  4. Test: Try syncing a test order and check the results in Xero.
  5. Go Live: Activate auto-sync and let x.ink handle the rest!
